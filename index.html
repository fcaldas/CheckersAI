What is CheckersAI

CheckersAI is a simple implementation of a Checkers game that allows you to play checkers against your computer. The project is still under devellopment but you can feel free to take a look on the source code and even clone the repository.

How can a computer play Checkers?

CheckersAI implements an well known algorithm in game theory that gives you the impression that you're playing against a decent player, the idea is to check all the possibilities that a movement can cause and then choose the one that minimizes our losses.
